About Alizon

Where medicine meets artificial intelligence.

Alizon School of Medical & Digital Intelligence was founded on a simple belief: the next generation of healthcare professionals must be fluent in both clinical practice and artificial intelligence.

We deliver industry-focused certification programs in AI, digital health and clinical technology — combining rigorous academics with browser-based virtual practicals and AI-scored assessments.
