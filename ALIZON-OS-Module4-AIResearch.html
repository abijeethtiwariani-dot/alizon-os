New to AI in research? Start here

AI can help you search literature, analyse data and draft papers — but its output must always be checked, and it must be used ethically. For each task:

  1. Know the research task — literature review, statistics, meta-analysis, writing.
  2. Verify the AI output — references (AI can invent them), statistics, and text must be independently confirmed.
  3. Apply the correct method — the right statistical test, PRISMA for reviews, the right model.
  4. Use AI responsibly — never fabricate data, plagiarise, or upload identifiable patient data; disclose AI assistance (AI is not an author).
