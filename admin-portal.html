Results

Publish module-wise results. Choose a course and module, then upload the results CSV. Students see them instantly on the public Results page (/alizon-results) by entering their registration number & batch. Re-uploading a module replaces it.

Publish a module result

CSV needs a registration-number column (header containing “reg”, “roll”, “admission” or “enroll”) and optionally a name column. Every other column (marks, grade, result…) is shown to the student.
