The Registration Desk

Register as a workshop delegate

The candidate profile below follows the Government skill-registry format (ASAP Kerala / Skill India candidate registration fields), so your details register you once for every Alizon workshop. Fields marked * are required.

1 · Personal details
2 · Identity
3 · Contact
4 · Domicile & permanent address
5 · Communication address
6 · Education & training
Your details are collected by the Alizon workshops office for workshop registration and, where applicable, candidate reporting to ASAP Kerala (Government of Kerala) in the official candidate-upload format. They are not shared with anyone else.
